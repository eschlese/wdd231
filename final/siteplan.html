Siteplan for the Kelowna Longboard Alliance

Site Name

The site name will be Kelowna Longboard Alliance. It was chosen because it is the name of the club, so it keeps things simple. If the domain was available, I would choose "kla.ca" for fast navigation to the site.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help those interested in longboarding in the Kelowna area to connect with the local club. The site will provide resources to help people contact the club, discover local events, and get involved in club activities.

Scenarios

1. How can I get in contact with the club? 2. Where and when are the clubs events/meet-ups? 3. How can I find people who have a similar interest in longboarding?

Colour Scheme

The primary colour will be #FFC251, the secondary colour will be #FFECCC, and the accent colour will be #476f3f. Text will be kept black for readability. The primary colour will be used for the header and accent colour for navigation. Most text background will use the secondary colour.

Typography

One font, Funnel Display, will be used for the whole page. This will keep the site looking clean and cohesive.
